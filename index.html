About Roshni

A pianist who teaches the way she wishes she'd been taught.

Roshni is a Trinity Grade 8 certified pianist who has spent 9+ years helping over 100 students fall in love with the piano — online and in person.

Rather than pushing every student through the same syllabus, she listens first: what do you want to play, how do you learn, and what would make practice feel like a pleasure rather than a chore? From there she builds lessons that balance technique, theory and real musicianship.

Patient, encouraging and genuinely invested in every student — from young children and adult beginners to neurodivergent learners who thrive with a flexible, personalised touch.
